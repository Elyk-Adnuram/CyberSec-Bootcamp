○ What do you think the risk is for individuals when their email addresses and passwords are exposed to the public?
There is a risk that bad actors can use the personal information of individuals in various types of cyber attacks
like email spam, phishing attacks as well as advance-fee fraud scams. The personal data can also be sold on the dark web 
to syndicates who then use the information perform identity theft and attempt to take out credit facilities or loans
in the name of the cyber crime victim.

○ Name two actions you can take to protect yourself against the risks associated with data breaches.
- Ensure that your passwords are very strong and regularly update your password. 
- Enable 2-factor authentication if it is available on a particular website or application.
